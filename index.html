Welcome to the Sukenik Lab!

We are part of the Department of Chemistry & Chemical Biology in the School of Natural Sciences at the University of California, Merced.

Our lab studies the interaction between proteins and their surrounding solutions in the test tube and in cells using a combination of live cell imaging, spectroscopy, and computational modeling.
